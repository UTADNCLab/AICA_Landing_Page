AI-Powered Collaborative Autonomy Challenge

The IEEE SMC AICA Challenge 2026 is a competition focused on the development of innovative solutions for collaborative autonomous systems. In this first year of the competition, the scenario features a multimodal autonomous delivery system consisting of a QCar2 ground vehicle and a QDrone2 aerial vehicle in a simulation environment.

Where Autonomy Meets Practical Delivery

As part of IEEE SMC 2026, held October 4–7, 2026, in Bellevue, WA, USA, the AICA Challenge provides a hands-on opportunity to develop innovative AI solutions using Quanser Interactive Labs (QLabs), the QCar2 ground vehicle, and the QDrone2 aerial vehicle platforms. The challenge is based on a realistic logistics scenario where packages are delivered from a pickup location to customers through path planning, control algorithms, and AI-driven decision-making.

Register

Submit

Compete
